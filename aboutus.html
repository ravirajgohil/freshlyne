Freshlyne is an Exporter, Importer and Worldwide Sourcing Company from India involved in Domestic market, export and import of day to day products. We are committed to bringing you the most excellent products along with the elevated benchmarks of quality at an ideal price.

Company is involved in trading various industry products, we offer Freshy made products by the experienced professionals, who do the process of tasting and quality check with the knowledge and experience in their respective fields. Add on to our advantage, Freshlyne have developed an exceptional relationship with Some of the leading manufacturing companies, which assist us in providing our clients with a best value. Company makes sure that we delight our clients every time with our quality product, Timely supply, accurate communication and professional approach.

We at Freshlyne live by the moto of “ Fresh Eating, Healthy Living” with the goal of providing organic & fresh Food products to the people. “Freshlyne” was established as a small business by four friends with the sole goal of providing the people with Pure & Hygienic food products ranging from Pulps, Sauces, Pickles, Juices etc.

Birth: When the whole world was reeling under the adverse effects of Corona virus (COVID19), we were planning to expand our business to fight this war with building the immunity of Human beings by providing them with Organic & hygienic food products that doesn’t compromise in taste. We came up with a plan to procure the best of each products under our umbrella & bring it to the people. Hence, the plan was executed by the name of “Freshlyne”. The name itself brings in the freshness & that healthy feeling.

Mission: Freshlyne aspires to take care of our clients need and believe in maintaining a genuine bond with them. Main activates undertaken in Freshlyne is direct and indirect export and import of various products with great quality.

Founders: Freshlyne was the brainchild of four friends with common goal of Creating a Healthy & Refreshing food products.
